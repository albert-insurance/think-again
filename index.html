    About us


    Stephanie Albert and her team are made up of licensed independent insurance brokers and financial advisors with over 28 years experience. We have strong ties to many top insurance companies and maintain professional and trusting relationships with our clients.

    We are life and living benefits specialists. In addition, we have furthered our expertise and received designations including, Elder Planning Counsellor (EPC), Registered Health Underwriter (RHU), Certified Health Specialist (CHS), Mental Health First Aid (MHFA). We are active members of the Independent Financial Brokers of Canada and MDRT and have been awarded and acknowledged many times for our professionalism and honesty by having our clients' best interests at heart.

    Stephanie and her team volunteer extensively in the community and support many different charities.
