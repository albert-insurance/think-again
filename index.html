    About us


    Stephanie Albert, and her business partner, Nad Khoja are both licensed independent insurance brokers and financial advisors for the past 20 years.

    Both are second generation brokers with strong ties to many insurance companies while maintaining professional and trusting relationships with their clients.

    Together, Stephanie and Nad are life and living benefits specialists. They have furthered their expertise and received designations such as, Elder Planning Counsellor (EPC), Registered Health Underwriter (RHU), Certified Health Specialist (CHS), Mental Health First Aid (MHFA). They are active members of the Independent Financial Brokers of Canada and MDRT. They have been awarded and acknowledged many time for their professionalism and honesty while having their clients best intentions at heart.

    Stephanie and Nad volunteer extensively in the community and support many different charities.
